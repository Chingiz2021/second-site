About our service

{% load static %} In the modern world, the relationship between humans and the environment is more important than ever. With Unwanted, you can learn about conscious consumption, recycling, and how we can reduce our carbon footprint by up to 75% just by reusing an item! Let s work together to make sure our planet stays healthy for generations to come.

After sorting, we give some of the things to those in need or sell them in second hand stores or partners, while others are sent for recycling.

We re committed to making sure our planet stays clean and healthy, which is why we strive to inform you on how to part with your unnecessary items with minimal harm to the environment. When we give items a second life, we not only prolong their life cycle but also help protect our environment in the process.
With small steps, we can change the planet for the better. But it's up to you to decide which direction that will be!
